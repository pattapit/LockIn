Monday A — Acceleration & Power

  • Sprints: 6–8 × 10–20y @ 90–100% effort. Full rest (60–90s) after each rep.
  • Plyo circuit (2 rounds):
    • Squat jumps: 6–8 reps
    • Pogo jumps (both legs): 20 reps
    • Skater jumps: 6 reps per side
    • Rest 45–60s between rounds
  • Optional finisher: 4 × 20y “fast but clean”. Rest ~45–60s.

Monday B — RSA (Soccer Gas)

  • Accelerations: 4 × 10y all-out. Focus on violent first 3 steps + forward lean. Walk back recovery.
  • RSA sets: 2–3 sets of 20 → jog back → 40 → jog back → 60 → jog back, then 90s rest between sets.
  • Short core (5 min circuit): rotate continuously for 5 minutes
    • Dead bugs: 10 per side
    • Side plank: 20–30s per side
    • Front plank: 30–45s
Dead bug = on your back, arms up, knees 90°. Extend opposite arm+leg while keeping low back flat.

Monday C — COD + Speed Endurance

  • COD: 6–8 reps of 5–10–5 or L-drill. Full rest (45–75s).
  • Speed endurance: 6 × 30s hard / 60s easy (hard = game-speed running with changes; easy = jog/walk).
  • Pogo jumps: 2 × 20 reps, 30–45s rest between sets.
