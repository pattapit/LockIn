The Athlete's Playbook

⚡ POWER (+3)

What: Chicken, Steak, Eggs, Carrots, Cukes, Peppers.

Why: Essential for repairing muscle fibers after training. High-density fuel that keeps your engine stable.

🍎 FRUIT (+2)

What: Berries, bananas, apples, mango.

Why: Nature's energy source. Provides quick fuel for the brain and muscles without the sugar crash.

🐢 SLOW BURN (+2)

What: Oats, Protein Pasta, Brown Rice, Quinoa.

Why: Sustained energy. This is the fuel that keeps you running in the 2nd half of a game.

🚀 FAST BURN (+1)

What: White Rice, Plain Pasta, Bagels, Potatoes.

Why: Immediate energy. Use these as a "top-off" before training or a game.

💧 WATER (Target: 8)

Why: Keeps the engine cool and the brain sharp for decision-making.

🍭 FUN FUEL (-3)

What: Candy, Soda, Junk Food.

Why: High spike energy that drains quickly. Fine for fun, but doesn't contribute to athletic recovery.
