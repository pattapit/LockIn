🖐️ Hand Portion Playbook

Use your hand to eye-ball a serving before logging. Small bites don't count!

PROTEIN / MEAT Open your hand and look at your palm. Think of your hand without fingers—one portion of meat should match the size and thickness of that palm.
VEGGIES (CARROTS/PEPPERS) Close your fist. Everything from your wrist up—that whole ball—makes one full serving of veggies.
PEANUT BUTTER One serving is roughly the size of your thumb. Two thumbs is a "Gold Medal" serving with fruit!
CARBS (RICE/PASTA) One serving is what fits inside one cupped hand (palm and fingers together making a bowl).
