The Athlete's Playbook

⚡ POWER (+3)

What: Chicken, Steak, Eggs, Carrots, Cucumbers, Peppers.

Why: Essential for repairing muscle fibers after training. High-density fuel that keeps your engine stable and prevents "heavy legs."

🍎 FRUIT (+2)

What: Berries, bananas, apples, mango.

Why: Nature's energy source. Provides quick fuel for the brain and muscles without a sugar crash.

🐢 SLOW BURN (+2)

The Tell: Usually brown, grainy, or hearty. Takes longer to chew and feels "heavy."

What: Oats, Protein Pasta (Barilla+), brown rice, quinoa, sweet potatoes, whole-wheat bread.

Why: Sustained energy. Like a big battery, this keeps you running and sprinting at full speed in the 2nd half of a game.

🚀 FAST BURN (+1)

The Tell: Usually white, fluffy, or soft. Melts in your mouth and digests fast.

What: White rice, bagels, plain pasta, uncrustables, white potatoes, pretzels.

Why: Immediate energy nitro boost. Great for right before training or at halftime.

💧 WATER (Target: 8)

Why: Essential for focus and temperature control. Keeps the brain sharp for making quick split-second decisions on the field.

🍭 FUN FUEL (-3)

What: Candy, Soda, Junk Food.

Why: High spike energy that drains quickly. Tells your body to store energy as fat rather than burn it for performance. Fine for fun—just balance it later!
